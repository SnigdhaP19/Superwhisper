Introduction

Remember how we keep on talking about hands-free typing?

Take it to the next level by getting Superwhisper to keep your recordings in your notes app!

Steps

  • Open up app
  • Press Record
  • Start talking
  • If it is your first time, you will need to authenticate. Rest assured! It's just verifying your apple ID
  • Click on notes
  • Select the notes app on your phone and send
