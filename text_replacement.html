Introduction

AI is not always perfect.

AI is continuously developing and constantly learning on our language-based interaction.

Luckily, Superwhisper is equipped with advanced technology and it has the option of text replacement.

Please note that this feature only works for certain words that Superwhisper constantly gets wrong.

Steps

  • Open Superwhisper
  • Go to Text Replacement
  • In the replace section, put the word you want to change
  • In the with section, put the word with the correct formatting of how it should print with
